Project context

Loop.com is a top-decile Shopify store: 3.79% CVR vs 1.4% industry average, 2M+ monthly active users, cart abandonment 9 points below norm. Years of disciplined CRO built this. We are not throwing it away.

The business is changing fundamentally. Loop is no longer a single-category earplug brand. We are becoming a multi-category sensewear brand — with new product tiers from €30 to €300+, new categories (Eclipse sleep mask, Cocoon kids earmuffs), major partnerships (McLaren F1, Harry Styles, Paris Hilton), and aggressive international expansion. A website built for one product at one price point cannot scale to this.

Job to be done

Build a website that reflects a world-class sensewear brand, helps every visitor find exactly what they need, scales effortlessly as we add categories and price tiers — and improves CVR in the process. Brand experience and conversion performance are not in tension here. Done right, they compound.

7 Design problems and KPI's
Exclusions
✕Brand refresh is out of scope. This project works within the existing brand identity. All rebrand implementation will happen only after BFCM.
✕New assets are out of scope. We will work only with existing assets, or as much as is possible within the limited time and resources.
✕Increased male traffic is not a success metric. The team will design for both men and women, but growing the male audience share is not a tracked outcome for this project.
Delivery approach

Phase 0 must complete before subsequent phases begin. Phases 1–3 run in sprint cycles by page — each page moves from Design Direction → Design → Experiment as quickly as possible. The majority of experiments must conclude before we build the Design System in Phase 4. This enables validated learning to flow directly into the harmonization pass.
